PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Populle is a free browser-based game. We respect your privacy and collect minimal data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. No registration, login, or email address is required to play.

Your game stats (best streak, games played, accuracy) are stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand how many people visit the site. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

Advertising

We may display advertisements through third-party ad networks. These services may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can learn more at Google's advertising policies page.

Cookies

Populle itself does not set cookies. Third-party advertising partners may set cookies. You can manage preferences through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Populle is a general-audience game. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Questions? See our About page.
