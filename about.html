ABOUT

What is Populle?

Populle is a free population comparison game. You're shown two things — countries, cities, US states, or wildcard entries — and you pick which has more people. Get it right and your streak continues. Get it wrong and it's game over.

How to Play

Tap the card you think has the higher population. If you're right, you move on to the next pair. If you're wrong, your streak ends. The smart pairing system ensures matchups are always interesting and debatable — never obvious.
